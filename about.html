About Change Moment

A practice built around careful listening and steady support.

We believe meaningful change often begins when people feel safe enough to be honest, curious, and supported.

Our story

Support that honors both difficulty and possibility.

Change Moment was created as a calm, welcoming psychotherapy practice for people who want care that is professional without feeling distant. Our clinicians bring a thoughtful, relational presence to each session.

The practice name reflects a belief that change is often made of moments: a clearer conversation, a new boundary, a deeper breath, a different way of understanding an old pattern.

Mission and values

Care shaped by respect, collaboration, and accessibility.

Respect

We recognize that each client brings a unique history, culture, identity, and pace.

Collaboration

Therapy goals are developed with you, not imposed on you.

Clarity

We aim to make the process understandable, transparent, and practical.

Credentials

Experience you can ask about.

  • Registered psychotherapy professionals with graduate-level clinical training.
  • Ongoing supervision, consultation, and continuing education.
  • Experience supporting adults, couples, families, and diverse communities.
  • Clear intake conversations about fit, scope, fees, confidentiality, and limits.

Therapy philosophy

Evidence-informed and deeply human.

Our therapists may draw from relational, emotion-focused, attachment-informed, CBT, mindfulness-based, and trauma-informed approaches. We adapt the work to your goals while staying honest about what therapy can and cannot provide.

What to expect

A thoughtful process from first contact onward.

Your first consultation is a chance to ask questions and get a sense of fit. If you begin therapy, early sessions often include clarifying goals, history, current stressors, and what support would feel useful.

Clear expectations

We discuss confidentiality, scheduling, fees, and communication practices.

Collaborative goals

We revisit what is helping and adjust the work as needed.

Respectful pacing

Difficult topics are approached with care and consent.

Connect with us

Questions about fit are welcome.
