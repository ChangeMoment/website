Welcome

Therapy that feels human, grounded, and practical.

At Change Moment, therapy begins with careful listening. We help clients make sense of patterns, build emotional capacity, and choose next steps that fit real life.

Our work is supportive and collaborative. We do not promise quick fixes, but we offer a steady place to explore what hurts, what matters, and what may be possible.

Who we help

For people seeking room to breathe and think clearly.

Clients often come to us when their usual ways of coping no longer feel enough. Therapy can help create space for reflection, choice, and support.

Anxiety and worry Relationship stress Burnout and overwhelm Grief and loss Life transitions Identity and self-worth

Our approach

Warm, evidence-informed, and paced with care.

Our therapists draw from relational, attachment-informed, cognitive, mindfulness-based, and trauma-informed practices. Your therapist will collaborate with you to shape the work around your goals and preferences.
