Therapy services

Support matched to your concerns, context, and pace.

Change Moment offers psychotherapy for individuals, couples, and families seeking a careful place to understand patterns and practice new possibilities.

Overview

Therapy can help you slow down, notice patterns, and choose what comes next.

The service areas below are common starting points. Your therapist will discuss fit, scope, and goals during consultation and intake.

Adults

Individual Therapy
