Therapy services

Support matched to your concerns, context, and pace.

Change Moment offers psychotherapy for individuals, couples, and families seeking a careful place to understand patterns and practice new possibilities.

Overview

Therapy can help you slow down, notice patterns, and choose what comes next.

The service areas below are common starting points. Your therapist will discuss fit, scope, and goals during consultation and intake.

Adults

Individual Therapy

One-to-one support for anxiety, self-criticism, grief, burnout, identity questions, and emotional overwhelm.

Relationships

Couples Therapy

Structured conversations for communication, recurring conflict, trust repair, intimacy concerns, and shared decisions.

Connection

Family Therapy

Support for families navigating transitions, communication stress, caregiving strain, and changing roles.

Nervous system

Anxiety and Stress

A place to understand worry, avoidance, pressure, and the habits that can form around stress.

Mood

Depression Support

Support for low mood, disconnection, motivation changes, and finding manageable next steps.

Paced care

Trauma-Informed Therapy

Careful, consent-based support for trauma impacts, emotional safety, and nervous system awareness.

Change

Life Transitions

Therapy for people navigating moves, career shifts, parenthood, separation, loss, cultural transitions, retirement, or a season of not knowing what comes next.

How it works

A clear path into care.

1. Consultation

2. Intake and goals
